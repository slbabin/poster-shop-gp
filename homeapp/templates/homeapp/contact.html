Frequently Asked Questions

Are your vintage posters authentic?

Yes, all of our vintage posters are authentic originals sourced from trusted suppliers and collectors. We take pride in offering genuine vintage pieces that reflect the charm and character of bygone eras.

Do you offer framing services for the posters?

No, at this time we do not offer framing service. But are planning to implement this service soon.

How do you package and ship the posters?

We take great care in packaging your vintage posters to ensure they arrive safely and in pristine condition. Each poster is securely rolled and packaged in a sturdy tube or flat mailer, depending on its size and format. We use reliable shipping carriers to deliver your order to your doorstep.

Can I return a vintage poster if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

Yes, we offer a hassle-free return policy. If you're not entirely satisfied with your vintage poster(s), you can initiate a return within 30 days of the delivery date. Please refer to our Return Policy page for more information on the return process and eligibility criteria.

Are the posters available in different sizes?

Yes, we offer vintage posters in various sizes to accommodate different preferences and display spaces. You can find the available sizes listed on the product page for each poster. If you have specific size requirements, please contact us, and we'll do our best to assist you.
