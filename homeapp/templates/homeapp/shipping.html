Shipping

Thank you for shopping at Poster Shop! We strive to provide you with the best possible shopping experience, including fast and reliable shipping options. Please review the following information regarding our shipping policies:

1. Shipping Rates and Delivery Times

We offer various shipping options to accommodate your needs. Shipping rates and delivery times may vary depending on the shipping method selected and your location. During the checkout process, you will be presented with available shipping options and their respective costs.

2. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Please note that processing times may vary during peak seasons or promotional periods. Once your order has been processed, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

3. Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: Delivery typically takes 2-3 business days.
  • Overnight Shipping: Delivery is guaranteed within 1 business day.

Please note that delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed. Actual delivery times may vary depending on factors such as carrier delays, weather conditions, and shipping destination.

4. Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to track the status of your shipment on our website or through the carrier's website.

5. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the total weight of your order and the selected shipping method. Shipping costs will be displayed during the checkout process before you complete your purchase.

6. Shipping Damage or Loss

In the unlikely event that your order arrives damaged or is lost during transit, please contact our customer service team immediately at [support@postershop-online.com]. We will work with you to resolve the issue and ensure your satisfaction.

If you have any further questions or concerns regarding shipping, please don't hesitate to contact us. Thank you for choosing Poster Shop!

Return policy

At Poster Shop, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with every purchase. If for any reason you are not entirely satisfied with your vintage poster(s), we offer a hassle-free return policy to make the process as simple and convenient as possible.

Our Return Policy Guidelines:

1. Eligibility:

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, undamaged, and in the same condition as when you received it. Returns must be initiated within 30 days of the delivery date.

2. Return Process:

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at contact@postershop.com to request a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. Once you have received your RMA number, securely package the item(s) you wish to return, including all original packaging materials and any accessories. Clearly mark the RMA number on the outside of the package. Ship the item(s) back to us using a trackable shipping method. Please note that customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

3. Inspection and Refund:

Upon receiving your returned item(s), our team will inspect them to ensure they meet our return policy guidelines. Once your return is approved, we will process your refund to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please allow up to 10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your financial institution's processing times.

4. Exclusions:

Custom-framed posters are not eligible for return unless they arrived damaged or defective. Items that have been altered, damaged, or tampered with after receipt are not eligible for return.

5. Damaged or Defective Items:

In the unlikely event that your vintage poster arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately to arrange for a replacement or refund. We may require photo evidence of the damage or defect for our records and to facilitate the return process.

6. Additional Information:

Original shipping charges are non-refundable. Refunds will be issued for the purchase price of the returned item(s) minus any applicable restocking fees, if applicable.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our return policy, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team for assistance. We're here to help make your shopping experience at Poster Shop as enjoyable as possible.
